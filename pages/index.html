Survive onslaughts of gruesome zombies to uncover who you can and can't trust in this action-packed multiplayer adventure extravaganza!

About the Game Creator Info

What Is This Game?

Friend No More is a strategic multiplayer zombie survival game where the player starts human but does not necessarily end that way. The zombies the player face mutate and evolve over time. Survival requires the player adapts just as much.

All it takes is one zombie bite to gain an advantage. More bites however, means more abilities, and also more insanity. Too many bites, and the player will shift from hunting zombies to hunting humans. Survival becomes a balance of humanity and the need to stay alive.

Only one player is allowed to win, so everything comes down to the actions and choices taken during the game. The winner however is not necessarily human and selected through a vote and dynamic point system. Friend No More is meant as a devious spin on an otherwise classic zombie genre.

Tips For Survival

  • To survive, you will need to know who to trust and when.
    You will be among friends and enemies, sometimes they are no different.
    The person right next to you could be alive, dead, or anywhere in between.

  • Survival through mutation is key.
    Survival requires that you take what you can, when you can, from whoever,
    at whatever cost.
    You must become the monster you are fighting against.

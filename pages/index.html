Friend No More

Survive onslaughts of gruesome zombies to uncover who you can and can't trust in this action-packed multiplayer adventure extravaganza!

About the Game Creator Details

What Is This Game?

Friend No More is a strategic multiplayer zombie survival game where the player begins human. As zombie waves progress, the zombies become more and more mutated. No zombie is alike. Zombies will become bigger and stronger and posess unknown and unique abilities through mutation. Players must adapt to the ever changing environment while they fight for survival. To surivive, the players must allow the zombies to mutate them. The player must get bitten to even out the playing field. The more bites however, the more zombie like the player becomes. The player must balance their humanity with their need to survive. If the player succumbs to too many bites, they will join the horde of zombies as whatever genetic-freak-mutation they've become and begin taking down the remaining humans and mutants alike. The game only allows one person to win, so it is a last man standing scenario where the last man standing isn't necessarily human. Friend No More is meant to be a devious spin on an otherwise classic zombie genre.

Tips For Survival

  • To survive, you will need to know who to trust and when.
    You will be among friends and enemies, sometimes they are no different.
    The person right next to you could be alive, dead, or anywhere in between.

  • Survival through mutation is key.
    Survival requires that you take what you can, when you can, from whoever,
    at whatever cost.
    You must become the monster you are fighting against.
